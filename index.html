    Watch the video to get more about Alfa School

    Established in 1990, Alfa English Medium High School is among the top K-10 institutes in Adoni, Andhra Pradesh. This co-ed school imparts BSEAP recognised education to students from Nursery to class 10th. Alfa English Medium High School facilitates its students and staff with top-class facilities including a transport facility along with a beautiful infrastructure at school. CCTV surveillance, well-trained security professionals, and fire safety systems are some of the top features contributing to a safe infrastructure. With the help of LEAD's learning management system, Alfa English Medium High School has maintained the quality of education even in the remote learning environment. For admission to Alfa English Medium High School, parents have to submit an enquiry form to get a detailed admission process from the officials.
